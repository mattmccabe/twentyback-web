Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TwentyBack

How quickly can I set up TwentyBack?

Most service professionals are up and running within 30 minutes. Our setup wizard guides you through connecting your calendar, creating your first automated responses, and customizing your booking preferences.

Will my customers notice it's automated?

TwentyBack's responses are professionally crafted and personalized with your business information. Customers appreciate the instant, professional communication.

What if I need to customize responses for different services?

TwentyBack allows customization. Built with flexibility in mind. Our platform allows human-in-the-loop collaboration for fine-tuning responses.

What does beta mean for me?

As a beta tester, you get exclusive early access to TwentyBack's automation features while they're being perfected. Your feedback directly influences development, and you'll receive lifetime benefits as a founding user.

How long will the beta last?

The beta program runs for approximately 3 months while we refine features based on real-world usage. Beta testers get grandfathered pricing and exclusive features when we launch publicly.

What happens to my data after beta?

All your data seamlessly transitions to the full platform. As a beta tester, you'll have priority support and your account will be automatically upgraded with special founding member benefits.

How can I provide feedback?

Beta testers have direct access to our founding team through weekly feedback sessions, a private Slack community, and priority support channels. Your input shapes every feature we build.

Is the beta free?

Yes! Beta access is completely free while we perfect the platform. Early testers will receive significant discounts and exclusive features when we launch our paid plans.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer live chat support, phone support, and a comprehensive knowledge base. Our team includes former service professionals who understand your business challenges and can provide practical advice.
